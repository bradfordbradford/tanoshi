Parking

Street parking on Elm, Main, and Commerce is metered, but free before 6pm during the week.

Directions

Directions from North Dallas/Plano/Frisco:

  • Head South on Central Expressway/US-75 S
  • Take exit 284C on the left towards Downtown/Good-Latimer
  • Turn left onto N. Good Latimer Expressway
  • Turn left onto Commerce St
  • Destination will be on the right

Directions from Arlington/Ft. Worth:

  • Head East on I30 towards Dallas
  • Take exit 45A on the left towards Commerce St/Downtown
  • Continue onto Commerce St
  • Destination will be on the right

Directions from Rockwall/Mesquite:

  • Head West on I30 towards Dallas
  • Take exit 47C to merge onto 1st Ave
  • Continue onto Exposition Ave
  • Turn left on to Canton St
  • Turn right onto Henry St
  • Turn right onto Commerce St
  • Destination will be on the right

Directions from South Dallas:

  • Head North on I-45 and continue onto US-75 N
  • Take exit 284B for Main St W
  • Merge onto Main St
  • Turn left onto S Pearl Expy
  • Take the 1st left onto Commerce St
  • Destination will be on the right
